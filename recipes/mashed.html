Mashed potatoes

Here is a delicious, simple recipe you can do at home.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Black olives(optional)
  • Sauseges

Steps:

  1. Mash the boiled potatoes.
  2. Add butter onto a hot pan.
  3. Add the mashed potatoes into the pan.
  4. Add a few sips of milk until it gets creamy.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients.
  6. It's possible to change sausages with any other ingredient of your choosing.

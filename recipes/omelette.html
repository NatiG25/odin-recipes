Omelette

A simple Omelette

Omelette are famous around the world, so obviously there would be thounsands of recipes out there. But this one is for the lazy one's.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Black olives
  • Tomatoes
  • Sweet pepper

Steps:

  1. Crack four Eggs.
  2. Add salt & black pepper.
  3. Mix it well.
  4. On a hot pan add one table spoon of Butter.
  5. Add freshly cut tomatoes, black olives & sweet pepper.
  6. After ten minutes of being cooked at low heat add the eggs.
  7. When the bottom of the eggs turns brown, flip one side.
  8. Bon Appetiet!

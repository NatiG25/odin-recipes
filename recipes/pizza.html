Pizza Margerita!

A delicious pizza that takes very short amount of time to make. It can be done effortlessly at home with common, easy to find ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sweet pepper
  • Black olives

Steps:

  1. Make a dough from wheat.
  2. Let it sit for half an hour.
  3. Spread it & make sure it's circular.
  4. Cover it with the remaining ingredients.
  5. Get a pan & add a few drops of oil.
  6. Get the dough into the pan, then put it inside the oven for about another half an hour at medium heat.
  7. Finally make sure to check every 10-15 minutes to make sure it's baked the way you like it.
